Who We Are

Born in Jaipur.
Worn everywhere.

Twin Shade started as a question: why does "streetwear" always feel like it was made for one kind of body? Dimple had been designing for years — bold prints, unexpected silhouettes — and the answer kept coming back the same. It didn't have to be this way.

So she built a brand that didn't. Every piece in every collection is cut for XS through 5XL. No compromises. No afterthoughts. Just fashion.

"We don't do 'plus-size as an afterthought.'
We do fashion, full stop."

— Dimple, Founder

The boho-artsy-street DNA isn't accidental. Jaipur bleeds colour. The city taught us that bold is not loud for the sake of it — it's a point of view. Twin Shade is that point of view, tailored and stitched with intention.

Every drop is limited. Every silhouette is intentional. Every size is real.
